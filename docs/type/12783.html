EnglishStorm LThe Storm is a mixed payload submunition system, designed as a versatile all-in-one solution for any combat scenario. Consisting of three different miniature warheads (EMP, Titanium And Plasma), it is incredibly hard to counter completely with standard defensive systems. The downside is of course that individually, the warheads are much weaker than normal and it's rather easy to counter at least part of the damage done. Can only be used by large tech level II+ Autocannons.
