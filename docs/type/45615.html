EnglishTengu Propulsion - Fuel CatalystThe ancient Sleeper race was known to have mastered various sustainable energy technologies including thermoelectric systems, which they usually built directly into a vessel's hull. Capsuleer reverse-engineers took little time to adapt salvaged versions of these Sleeper energy systems into their own modular Tech III vessel pieces. Thanks to widespread demand in the Caldari transport industry, fuel catalyst systems were one of the first to be developed.
