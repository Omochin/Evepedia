EnglishPlutonium Charge XLExtra Large Hybrid Charge. Can be used only by starbase defense batteries and capital ships like dreadnoughts. Consists of two components: a shell of titanium and a core of plutonium atoms suspended in plasma state. Railguns launch the shell directly, while particle blasters pump the plasma into a cyclotron and process the plasma into a bolt that is then fired. 37.5% reduced optimal range. 5% reduced capacitor need.

FrenchCharge de plutonium XLTrès grande charge hybride. Exploitable uniquement par les batteries de défense de base stellaire et les vaisseaux capitaux tels que les dreadnoughts.
