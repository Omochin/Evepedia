Whilst in the deployed configuration, energy from the Rorqual's engines is channeled into incredible shield defenses, improved mining foreman bursts and significantly enhanced mining drone coordination. Deployed Rorquals also gain access to special assembly lines which can compress asteroid ore and ice. Note: Can only be fitted on the Rorqual ORE capital ship.
