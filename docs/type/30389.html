EnglishSubsystem Assembly ArrayA mobile assembly facility where advanced subsystems and hulls of strategic cruisers can be manufactured. This structure has no specific time or material requirement bonuses to subsystem manufacturing. Note: Tech III hulls cannot be assembled at starbase structures. The hulls and subsystems can only be assembled whilst docked in a station.
