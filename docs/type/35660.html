English50MN Quad LiF Restrained MicrowarpdriveMassive boost to speed for a very short time. The thrust that boosts the ship, and the corresponding maximum velocity bonus, are limited by the mass of the ship that uses this module. Penalty: Max capacitor reduced. Note: Usually fit on Cruisers and Battlecruisers.
