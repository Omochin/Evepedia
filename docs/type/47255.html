EnglishEFFA Compact Assault Damage ControlUtilizes a combination of containment field emitters and redundancy systems to mitigate the impact of critical system damage. Grants a bonus to resistance for shield, armor and hull. May be activated to grant extreme resistances to shield, armor and hull for a short period of time. Only one Damage Control can be fit at a given time. Assault Damage Controls can only be fit to Assault Frigates and Heavy Assault Cruisers.

GermanEFFA Compact Assault Damage ControlVerwendet eine Kombination aus Dämpfungsfeldemittern und Redundanzsystemen um die Auswirkungen von kritischen Systemschäden zu verhindern.
